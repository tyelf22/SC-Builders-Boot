WHO IS SC BUILDERS?

SC Builders, Inc., is a full service General Contractor based in Grantsville, Utah. Founded in 1997 and built on a foundation of honesty, integrity, value, and efficiency. We have grown to become a top General Contractor in the Tooele and Salt Lake Valley.

BUILT FOR LIVING

At SC Builders, we help you turn your house into a home. If you can dream it, then we can build it. We strive to build lasting homes and lasting relationships with our customers. Choose SC Builders and we promise you will feel that there is no place like home.

OUR MISSION

To provide the people of Tooele County with their dream home and dream building experience.
